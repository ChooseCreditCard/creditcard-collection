FAQ about The CreditCard Collection

Can I acquire a single domain, or only the full collection?

Both are possible. The strongest story is to keep the .creditcard namespace together, but individual names can be carved out for the right strategic fit.

Who is this built for?

Primarily fintech founders, card issuers, neobanks, Web3 payment teams and B2B expense platforms who want a namespace that clearly communicates “cards and payments”.

Do these domains come with live products?

The collection is product-ready but not tied to a specific issuer stack. That gives a buyer the freedom to plug in their own processing, BIN sponsor and compliance framework.

Where can I see the consumer-facing hub?

The comparison hub lives on Choose.Creditcard, while the network overview and sitemap live on Network.Creditcard.
